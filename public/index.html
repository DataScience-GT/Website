About

Data Science at Georgia Tech aims to provide students with a platform to learn about Data Science and develop Project Management skills

What Do We Do?

We are a student-run organization at Georgia Tech driven by the four goals:

  • Promote, inform, and educate on data science trends
  • Use real-world data to solve real-world problems
  • Partner with top companies for informational and recruitment events and other groups like GT Police Department for non-profit projects
  • Host and sponsor Data Science hackathon during Spring 2019

Aside from the above, Data Science at GT aims to develop technical skills in data science and analytics for students at Georgia Tech. Through various activities such as the construction of a data science blog, inviting guest speakers and professors, and discussing topics in the field, the organization will serve as a platform for students to learn about and practice data science and analytics. It will also give students the opportunity to network with like-minded individuals and professionals through events and information sessions.
